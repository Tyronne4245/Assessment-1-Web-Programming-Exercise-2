Overview

Hollow Knight is a 2017 Metroidvania video game developed and published by Australian independent developer, Team Cherry. The player controls the Knight, an insectoid warrior exploring Hallownest, a fallen kingdom plagued by a supernatural disease.

Characters

  • The Knight - A discarded vessel. They are the child of the Pale King and the White Lady, born in the abyss with void insider their shell.

  • Hornet - The daughter of the Pale King and Herrah the Beast, the queen of Hollownest. Her birth was the result of a bargain for her mother to become a Dreamer, and as such, she spent only a short time with Herrah.

  • Zote the Mighty - A traveller from outside Hallownest who came to the Kingdom to fulfill a promise of glory for himself.

  • The Dreamers - Three beings who entered into an eternal sleep in order to use their power to seal away the Hollow Knight. The ones who became the Dreamers are Monomon the Teacher, Lurien the Watcher, and Herrah the Beast.

  • The Pale King - A higher being who used to be the monarch of Hallownest, mate to the White Lady, and ruler of the White Palace.

  • The Radiance - A higher being of light similar to Essence, and as such, opposed to the Void, her ancient enemy. The Moth Tribe is born from her light and in return revered her.

  • Sealed Vessel - The Vessel chosen by the Pale King to seal away the Radiance and save Hallownest from the Infection.

  • Grimm - The master of the Grimm Troupe, a mysterious travelling circus. In truth, Grimm and his Troupe travel from the Nightmare Realm to wherever the Nightmare Lantern has been lit by acolytes.

  • The Seer - The last surviving member of the Moth Tribe. She tends the graves of the Resting Grounds and guides the Knight because she regrets that her ancestors forgot about their creator, the Radiance.

  • Quirrel - An inquisitive, up-beat explorer, fascinated by the myths of Hallownest for reasons he cannot explain.
